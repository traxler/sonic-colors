Design Rationale

Creative Professionalism

The Plus Jakarta Sans and Figtree pairing strikes a perfect balance between creative expression and professional reliability.

Plus Jakarta Sans provides a structured, confident foundation for our interface with its clean geometric forms. The subtle rounded corners add just enough approachability without sacrificing professionalism.

Figtree complements this with its warm, humanist qualities. The open letterforms and generous spacing create excellent readability for longer content like project descriptions and documentation.

Together, they embody our brand values: innovation meets accessibility, professionalism meets creativity.

Accessibility

This typography system exceeds WCAG 2.1 AA standards with these accessibility features:

  • Optimal x-height: Both fonts have tall x-heights, improving character recognition
  • Character distinction: Clear differentiation between similar characters (1/l/I, 0/O)
  • Weight contrast: Heading/body weight ratio of 600:400 provides clear hierarchy
  • Line length control: Max 70 characters per line for readability
  • Line height: 1.6 for body text enhances scanning
  • Size progression: Clear heading scale establishes information hierarchy

The system maintains 4.5:1 contrast ratio for body text and 3:1 for headings against backgrounds in both light and dark modes.

User Experience

This typography system enhances our SaaS experience in several key ways:

  • Quick scanning: Heading hierarchy helps users navigate complex interfaces
  • Reduced fatigue: Figtree's open forms and spacing minimize reading fatigue during long sessions
  • Clear affordances: Button text uses Jakarta Sans at 600 weight for clear CTAs
  • Contextual emphasis: Strategic use of weight and color directs attention
  • Multi-device rendering: Fonts maintain clarity on all screen densities

The combination creates a rhythm that guides users through workflows while maintaining the creative energy essential to music production.
